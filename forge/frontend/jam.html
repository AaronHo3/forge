◆ Live Morph / Jam

MRT2 plays a continuous, evolving stream. Steer it live and morph between styles as it plays.

Idle - press Start (first start loads MRT2, ~10s).
Move the Morph slider while it plays - you'll hear A blend into B in real time. Edit the prompts and hit Apply to steer it somewhere new. Capture saves the recent audio to your Library, where you can Restyle it or send it to Audiotool.
