Journey Composer

Compose the arc, not the notes - MRT2 performs your evolving structure live, then you capture it.

← Workbench
Design an arc, then press Perform (first run loads MRT2, ~10s).
Each waypoint is a destination - a vibe (prompt), a busyness (density), and how many seconds to morph into it. MRT2 plays continuously and glides through your waypoints in order. Capture saves the performance to your Library for Restyle / Audiotool.
